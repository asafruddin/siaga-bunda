# Input Reconciliation — Product Brief and Addendum

## Coverage

- The PRD preserves the brief’s educational-not-diagnostic boundary and urgent-care escape path.
- Research, ethics, consent, privacy, clinical-safety, security, and incident readiness are explicit pilot gates.
- Withdrawal without penalty, consent comprehension, privacy-safe reminders, pseudonymous monitoring, controlled export, immutable Source Records, corrections, and versioned materials are represented as requirements.
- Low connectivity, device switching, clock manipulation, shared-device risk, accessibility, notification denial, and Protocol Deviations are explicit behaviors or test targets.
- User and research success metrics avoid equating completion or watch duration with comprehension or clinical effect.

## Remaining external gates

The PRD keeps seven questions open because they require accountable human or institutional approval. They are parameterized so UX and architecture can proceed, but real recruitment and production Study Data remain blocked until resolved.
