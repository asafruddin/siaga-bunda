# UX Reconciliation — `docs/blueprint.md`

The blueprint’s respondent and Researcher screen concepts are preserved as flows rather than copied screen-for-screen. The UX adds the missing safety and recovery surfaces required by the final PRD: informed Consent, comprehension, Urgent-care Guidance, withdrawal, offline and stale state, permission denial, interrupted Video reconciliation, content suspension, re-consent, Researcher suppression, controlled export, and secure session recovery.

The blueprint’s soft maternal-health direction is retained as calm, warm, accessible visual language without assuming a decorative illustration style or pink gender coding. Researcher UX remains mobile-first.
