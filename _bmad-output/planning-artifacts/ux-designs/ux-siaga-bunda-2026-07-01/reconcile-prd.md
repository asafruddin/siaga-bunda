# UX Reconciliation — Final PRD

## Covered

- UJ-1 through UJ-5 appear as named Key Flows with entry, numbered steps, climax, and failure path.
- Participant and Researcher needs have explicit IA surfaces; all 36 FRs are represented by a surface, component, state, interaction rule, or internal operational boundary.
- Loading, empty, error, offline, stale, locked, waiting, notification-denied, interrupted Video, duplicate submission, withdrawal, content suspension, re-consent, export, and authorization states are explicit.
- Consent comprehension, optional-purpose separation, urgent-care escape, pseudonymous monitoring, controlled export, immutable Source Records, 168-hour Delay, backend authority, and Android-only scope remain intact.

## Deliberate UX decisions

- Material 3 conventions with a SiAGA Bunda visual layer.
- Light-mode-only tested MVP; dark mode deferred until semantic contrast is verified.
- Minimal bottom navigation per role; no drawer and no casual role switch.
- Internal Study configuration/publication remains controlled operational tooling rather than a mobile end-user surface.

## External content dependencies

- Exact Consent, comprehension, eligibility, urgent-care, support, withdrawal, and clinical copy remain versioned Study/Clinical Owner artifacts.
