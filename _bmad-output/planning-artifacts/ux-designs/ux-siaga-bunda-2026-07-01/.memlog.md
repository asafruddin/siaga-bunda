- (decision) Use an Android-only, Material 3-conformant mobile experience because the finalized PRD explicitly narrows the MVP platform.
- (decision) Adopt a calm teal Participant palette, blue Researcher accent, warm neutral surfaces, and reserved danger/warning/success semantics; avoid infantilizing pink, gamification, and clinical reassurance.
- (decision) Use role-separated navigation: Participant Beranda/Profil and Researcher Ringkasan/Peserta/Ekspor, with no casual role switch or shared cached navigation.
- (decision) Keep Urgent-care Guidance one tap away from every Participant task and bundle an approved baseline for first-launch offline access.
- (decision) Use exact localized date, time, and timezone for the 168-hour Delay; refresh backend state at the boundary rather than trusting device time.
- (decision) Use neutral Indonesian microcopy with Anda by default; Ibu plus name requires approved content and participant preference.
- (decision) Treat notification permission as optional and contextual after Video Completion; Dashboard availability is the authoritative fallback.
- (decision) Protect local drafts through encryption, account/version scope, expiry, and deletion on acceptance, withdrawal, logout, role change, or revocation.
- (decision) Make every IA surface spine-only in this headless run; no mockups or imported brand assets were produced, and the two spines win on conflict.
- (change) Edge-case review added first-launch offline, ineligibility, re-consent, required update, storage failure, offline withdrawal, mid-playback suspension, concurrent device, and task-switcher privacy states.
- (event) spines finalized after PRD/blueprint reconciliation, rubric validation, contrast checks, edge-case remediation, and editorial polish
